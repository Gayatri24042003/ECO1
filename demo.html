ABOUT

The Ecotrack aims to revolutionize waste disposal and recycling through cutting-edge technology. By analyzing collection data, we provide valuable insights into waste volume per region, the effectiveness of waste segregation, and overall system efficiency.

Data Analysis

Our data-driven approach ensures efficient resource allocation, optimized collection schedules, and informed decisions to improve overall waste management practices.

GPS Tracking

We use GPS-enabled vehicles to monitor waste collection routes in real-time, ensuring timely pickups and minimizing the carbon footprint.

Collection Time Prediction

Through GPS tracking, Ecotrack can estimate and provide real-time waste collection times, allowing communities to better manage their disposal practices.

Automated Reporting

Our system generates automated reports on waste collection efficiency, providing stakeholders with actionable insights to refine and improve sustainability efforts.
