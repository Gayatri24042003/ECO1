WASTE MANAGEMENT RULES

Understanding the rules of waste management is crucial for a sustainable future. Here are some important guidelines to follow:

1. Segregate Waste

Always separate your waste into biodegradable and non-biodegradable categories to promote effective recycling and composting.

2. Dispose Properly

Use designated bins for different types of waste and ensure that hazardous materials are disposed of at specific collection points.

3. Reduce Plastic Use

Minimize the use of single-use plastics. Opt for reusable bags, containers, and bottles to lessen waste.

4. Participate in Recycling Programs

Engage with local recycling initiatives and educate yourself about proper recycling practices to enhance community efforts.

5. Compost Organic Waste

Create a composting system at home for organic waste to reduce landfill contributions and produce natural fertilizer.

6. Educate Others

Spread awareness about waste management rules and practices within your community to foster a culture of sustainability.
